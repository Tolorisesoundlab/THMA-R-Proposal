Why THMA&R matters

Company Overview

THMA&R is an NGO operating under Tolorise Sound Lab (Pty) Ltd, focused on music education, artist development, and cultural upliftment across South Africa and beyond.

The organisation combines a professional academy and record label model to support emerging talent through training, mentorship, production, and opportunities that build long-term creative careers.

Core Activities

  • Music education and training
  • Artist development and management
  • Recording, mixing, and mastering
  • Music publishing and digital distribution
  • Event and talent showcase management

Vision & Mission

Purpose-driven creative growth

Vision

To become Africa’s most respected music academy and independent record label, producing globally recognized artists while preserving and promoting African heritage through music.

Mission

Discover talent, deliver professional mentorship, produce international-standard music, promote African creativity, and create sustainable career opportunities.

Impact

Build a powerful ecosystem of learning, performance, production, and commercial release for artists and music professionals across Africa.

Growth Plan

Objectives across the next decade

Years 1–2

Launch academy operations, enroll 100 students annually, sign 5–10 artists, release 20 projects, and build a digital presence.

Years 3–5

Expand to 500 students, build Southern African partnerships, launch annual showcases, and create sustainable revenue from education, music, and events.

Years 5–10

Develop a dedicated music campus, establish international distribution, launch the THMA Heritage Music Festival, and become an African talent-development institution.
